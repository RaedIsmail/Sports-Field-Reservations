Soccer Court

Seattle Parks and Recreation has 200+ athletic fields suitable for seasonal, occasional and tournament play for sports such as, but not limited to; softball, baseball, soccer, football, ultimate, lacrosse and more. Thirty of the fields are synthetic surfaced which allows for year round play and 59 are lighted. For one time field reservations, adult practice field users and first time league requests, please use the link above for an application and follow the instructions shown.
Historical leagues do not need to use this application. Fields will be rolled over automatically from season to season. If major changes are needed for a season please e-mail that information to the field scheduler you normally communicate with.

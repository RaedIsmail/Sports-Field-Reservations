Amy Yee Tennis Center Facility Improvements Tennis Center Re-Opens November 9, 2019. Indoor Court Reservations may be made starting November 7 by calling 206-684-4764. To celebrate the improvements and re-opening, Seattle Parks and Recreation (SPR) is offering a free Grand Re-Opening Tennis Clinic on November 9, from 10 a.m. to 1:45 p.m. and Open Match Play on November 10, from 10 a.m. to 3 p.m. The community is encouraged to attend the free tennis activities. The majority of improvements are complete; however, the new main lobby entrance doors and windows will be installed on Monday, November 25 and Tuesday, November 26, requiring the facility to be closed until 5 p.m. both days. The facility will be open and reservable for tennis from 5 p.m. to 10:30 p.m. on both those days.

Where a participant entry fee for tournaments or a fee for lessons is charged, 10% of gross receipts is to be paid to the Department in addition to the charges noted below. All persons who instruct tennis for a fee on Department courts must schedule according to court availability with the Scheduling Office, obtain the appropriate permit and pay the required fees. They must also successfully complete the Department's Tennis Instructor's Certification program and show proof of certification before a permit will be issued.
